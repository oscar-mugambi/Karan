Karibu Nyumbani - Welcome Home
“Karan” means “good” or “beautiful” in the Kalenjin language - one of Kenya’s tribes, and perhaps the world’s most athletic community. Karan Africa, therefore, stands for “beautiful Africa”. This name best illustrates the continent’s rich, vibrant, diverse and authentic culture!

Who Can Travel with Us?

1. Annual Vacationers

Are you taking a break from work, business or school? Do you want to travel alone, as a pair or with friends or family? Let’s tailor-make a refreshing yet experiential trip.

2. Travel enthusiasts

Are you about globetrotting and want to experience culture while seeing the world? We would like to give you an experience you have not had before!

3. Families

Gift your family with the ultimate African trip that all will remember for a long time. We will develop a special trip for you that will present a different perspective of life.

4. Retirees

Are you a retiree seeking an experience that will shape the next chapter of your life? How about packing up and travelling to Africa! Not as a typical tourist, but for a taste of cultural tourism. We promise you a trip to remember!

5. Lovebirds

How about a trip for you and your partner where you get to experience love in the African context. Not your typical candlelit dinner but rather under the stars in the countryside. How about being a part of mock customary ceremonies? Come have a blend of adventure and traditional lessons on love and life while creating wonderful memories.

6. Church & social groups

Is your group seeking a life-altering mission? Come travel with us. We will expose you to a whole new world and give you the opportunity to impact on communities.

7. School & college leavers

Are you looking for a place to spend your Spring break or Summer holiday? Are you waiting to join college or university? How about having fun while exploring other cultures? Let us know and we will hook you up with holiday that will be the envy of your friends.

8. Philanthropists

We organize excursions for committed philanthropists that want to make genuine impact on communities. We will work with you in your area of interest to address issues that are close to your heart.

9. Learning institutions & students

When culture is experienced the lessons learned stick better than theoretical concepts. We facilitate excursions for institutions and students of culture that want to gain first-hand exposure to the same. We work with local cultural and academic partners with in-depth subject knowledge to make your trip both informative and enjoyable.
