Africa is not a country

Africa is not a country but a continent with 54 countries.
With around 3,000 tribes and 2,000 languages and dialects,
Africa is the most diverse continent in the world.

Africa is enormous

Africa is the second largest continent on earth with about 1.4 billion people, contributing to close to 17% of the world’s population. In fact Africa is huge, so huge it can literally fit China, US, India and some parts of Europe based on landmass.

Multiculturalism

There are believed to be around 3,000 different African tribes and all of them have their own distinct cultures, traditions and customs.

Multilingualism

There are more than 2,000 distinct languages in the African continent
the most popular being Swahili, Yoruba and Zulu.

Independence

All of Africa was colonized by foreign powers during the “scramble for Africa”, except Ethiopia and Liberia.
