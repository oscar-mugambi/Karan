Our Travels

Travelling means more to us than just seeing places. Travel is truly memorable when it involves meaningful engagements and elements of experiential learning. A big part of our expeditions are away from the big cities, with limited influence of modern life, where you get to experience life in a traditional and authentic manner. We expose you to traditional cultural, social and economic activities that are still practiced by the local people. To fully immerse and be part of the community, we prioritize homestays in traditional homesteads as much as possible.

Would you like to listen to century-old tales while enjoying a bonfire? How about joining in traditional music and dance? Ever thought of enjoying home-brewed traditional beer. Karan gives you the chance to experience all that comes with staying with a local family, gelling in the culture and sharing their lives. Africans have a reputation for warmth and hospitality. There is perhaps no better a way to experience this than to be part of their daily lives and activities for a few days. Come join us and write your African tale as you explore amazing places and have life-changing experiences.

Beyond the cultural experience, we let you have a taste of the standard tourism circuits. Get to see the Big-5, enjoy a long safari or take a short drive through the national park within a city. Have a meal with the giraffes, feed and bath orphaned elephants, take a day hike up some rugged hills or challenge yourself by summiting a mountain. You can choose to sample the white sandy beaches or possibly a night out in the city before your trip back home. We tailor what best suits you and guarantee a memorable experience!
