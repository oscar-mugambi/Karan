Karibu Nyumbani - Welcome Home!
“Karan” means “good” or “beautiful” in the Kalenjin language - one of Kenya’s tribes, and perhaps the world’s most athletic community. Karan Africa, therefore, stands for “beautiful Africa”. This name best illustrates the continent’s rich, vibrant, diverse and authentic culture! The birth of Karan Africa was inspired by a desire to present the best of Africa to the world in its truest form! The beauty is in the people, the culture and heritage, the food and drinks, the language, the music and dance. It’s in the natural sceneries and rugged landscapes, from the rainforests to the deserts, from the sandy white beaches to the snow-capped mountains. It’s in the glorious marine and land wildlife; from the historic sites to the modern-day cities. Africa is truly beautiful and waiting to be explored.
Karan welcomes you to join us in the exploration of the continent, going off the beaten track to have an unfettered experience of this gem. We design unique and fascinating trips which you as a traveler will be intrigued to be part of. You will get to sample the real and pure Africa while mingling with local folk. Your African trip will be unforgettable experience of cultural immersion blended with archetypal tourism.
