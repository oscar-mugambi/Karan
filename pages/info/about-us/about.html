ABOUT US
Karan is a Kenyan-based company with a reach across the African continent. The untold real stories of Africa and its people are immense and unending. As your travel partner, we seek to explore the beauty and authenticity of our vast cultures and give you the opportunity to immerse yourself in it. Ours is to explore, share and enlighten others on what has either been misconceived or is not yet told. We partner with other curious travelers who are keen on new experiences away from the standard tourism. People who are ready to go off the beaten track and experience life as a local. We envision a world that appreciates how different we all are while allowing a pure human connection with each other. Karan’s inspiration lies in the connection made out of cultural exchanges and sharing of each other’s lives in the process. We offer real local experiences to socially aware, culturally curious and adventure seeking travelers who are looking for something genuine, unique and memorable to indulge in. We strive to make traveling a meaningful and profound social experience. Our reach includes non-Africans who want to delve and soak in the African spirit, as well as Africans who want to retrace their roots and engage with their past. We welcome you to a life-changing type of travel.
