Fact One

Africa is not a country but a continent with 54 countries.

Fact Two

Africa is the second largest continent on earth with about 1.4 billion people, contributing to close to 17% of the world’s population.

Fact Three

There are believed to be around 3,000 different African tribes and all of them have distinct own cultures, traditions and customs

Fact Four

There are more than 2,000 distinct languages in the African continent.

Fact Five

All of Africa was colonized by foreign powers during the “scramble for Africa”, except Ethiopia and Liberia.
