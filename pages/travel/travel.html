Our Travels

Travelling means more to us than just seeing places. Travel is truly memorable when it involves meaningful engagements and elements of experiential learning. A big part of our expeditions are away from the big cities, with limited influence of modern life, where you get to experience life in a traditional and authentic manner.

Our Travels

We expose you to traditional cultural, social and economic activities that are still practiced by the local people. To fully immerse and be part of the community, we prioritize homestays in traditional homesteads as much as possible.
We partner with other curious travelers who are keen on new experiences away from the standard tourism. People who are ready to go off the beaten track and experience life as a local. We envision a world that appreciates how different we all are while allowing a pure human connection with each other. Karan’s inspiration lies in the connection made out of cultural exchanges and sharing of each other’s lives in the process. We offer real local experiences to socially aware, culturally curious and adventure seeking travelers who are looking for something genuine, unique and memorable to indulge in. We strive to make traveling a meaningful and profound social experience. Our reach includes non-Africans who want to delve and soak in the African spirit, as well as Africans who want to retrace their roots and engage with their past. We welcome you to a life-changing type of travel.
